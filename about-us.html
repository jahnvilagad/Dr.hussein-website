About Me

Dr. Razan Hussein is a GDC-registered dentist with 9 years of experience and has obtained a Master in Clinic Dentistry in Restorative and Cosmetic Dentistry. Dr Hussein has gained a wide range of generic skills and developed an interest in Maxillofacial Surgery including pedodontics, prosthodontics and Conservation as well as outstanding skill knowledge and experience in Periodontology and Implantology.

Her experience of living in several different places and countries has led to excellent people skills and language fluency in both English and Arabic. Dr. Hussein is a competent and self-motivated professional, highly innovative, and adaptable, with a solution-focused and collaborative approach to cosmetic dentistry and patients. Her excellent organisational and communication skills allow her to work seamlessly amongst the team and with her patients to work towards outstanding cosmetic results and patient satisfaction.

Dr Hussein is currently published her dissertation and in her spare time enjoys travelling, playing with her 2 little kids,cinema and keep fit and healthy with exercise.
